Case Studies

The following case studies, drawn from across existing CVE literature, seek to demonstrate the efficacy of past and present counter-messaging strategies on violent non-state movements that share common factors with Boko Haram and Northern Nigeria. In assessing each addition, the best practices team weighed variables including race, ethnicity, geography, rates of development, internet access, education and literacy, religion and religious extremism, and state military strength. While the case studies vary in their respective comparability to Boko Haram, each possesses important insights as to why and how individual insurgents choose to disengage from extremist organizations.
